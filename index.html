Operating Systems Comparison

Having the right operating system for gaming is a major advantage for gaming performance.Here is a comparison of the two most popular operating systems and their relavant advantages and disadvantages. Please hover over the card.

  • Windows Computers are more likely have better graphics cards than Mac Computers.
  • Nowadays, many modern games may not be available in Mac computers.
  • Windows Computer's Microsoft Store Library is making easier for users toto access and download games.
  • Windows computers may now be much harder for new gamers/computer users to use than the Mac computers.
  • In the long run, windows computers are just the better choice for a pc gamer.
  • Mac Computers maybe easier to navigate applications and websites for new gamers/computer users than Windows computers.
  • Many Mac computers have an internal integrated graphics card which may make the gaming experience slower compared to Windows Computers which has access to external graphics card.
  • Mac Computers maybe better for running everyday applications and boots faster than a Windows Computer.
  • On average,Mac Computers tend to be more expensive than Windows Computers.

Recommended Gaming Store

A Gaming Store is a library of games offered by many third party business, both free and paid.The gaming store help many of these third party businesses promote and sell their games to consumers in both in a safe and secure way. Here is a list of the most popular libraries.

Steam

Steam is developed by Valve Corporation. It is currently the most popular gaming library and store. Currently available in both Mac and Windows.

Microsoft Store

Not to be mixed with the Microsoft store at your local mall, this Microsoft Store is included in Windows 10 Computers. The store offers more than games such as entertainment, music, and writing applications.

Epic Games

The Epics Game store is known for its popular cross platform game called Fornite. A cross platform game is a game that allows pc gamers and console gamers play together despite system differences. The Epic Games Store is available in both Mac and Microsoft.
