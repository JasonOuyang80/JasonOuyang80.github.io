What is Steam?

Steam is everything for gaming. It is a store which include paid or free games from third party companies, a destination to play games, and a place to connect and make friends. This is a great place for new gamers as they can explore, make connections with other people, and securely download and install games.

Tutorial to install and Use Steam

Step 1

Register for a Steam Account here You will need a steam account to access the Steam Application.Also, make sure your account is verified by accessing your email after sign up.

Access email and find a email from steam in inbox.Make sure you click the verification link so the signup process goes smoothly.

Step 2

Download and Install Steam here. Click on the download link.

Please make sure you have enough space in your hard drive to download steam since many of the games from steam will be downloaded into the current hard drive.
