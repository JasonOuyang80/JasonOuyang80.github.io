What is Steam?

Steam is everything for gaming. It is a store which include paid or free games from third party companies, a destination to play games, and a place to connect and make friends. This is a great place for new gamers as they can explore, make connections with other people, and securely download and install games.

Tutorial (Windows 10 Computer)

Step 1

Register for a Steam Account here.Remmember your username and password! You will need a steam account to access the Steam Application.Also, make sure your account is verified by accessing your email after sign up.

Now, Access your email account and find a receiving email from Steam in your inbox. Open it and click the verification link so the signup process is authenticated. This has to be done right after your register for an account.

Step 2

Download and Install Steam here.The link will take you the steam website to download the Steam installer.

Please make sure you have enough space in your hard drive to download steam since many of the games from steam will be downloaded into the current hard drive.

Now it is time to access the Steam Application. The best way is to find it is through the search bar at the bottom of your screen.

Step 3

It will now load the Steam Login Screen. You will now enter the password and username you entered during registration. Save password if this is your personal computer.

Congradulations! Now you have Steam on your Computer. The Steam Application should look like this. (Please ignore the games)

Imporant Things to Know

Steam Friends

This is your friends messenger application which comes with Steam. It allows you to communicate with friends also with the steam application. Think of it as another social messenger application.

Steam Store

This where you are able to search and purchase or get for free, games that are from third party companies. Think of Steam as an apple store or an google app store. They ensure the games are completly up to date, legal, and free from any viruses.

Steam Library

This is where your acquired games will be located. The benefits of this Library is that the games does not have to be installed on your computer to show up here. You can choose to install the game you would like to play here.
